About Me

No corporate-speak, promise.

I'm a data person who got there sideways — MBA first, then an MSc in Data Science once I realised business strategy without good data underneath it is just a guess with extra confidence. These days I split my time between data governance, cost analytics, and whatever new tool I've decided to learn that week (dbt, currently).

What I actually care about: data you can trust, decisions you can explain, and pipelines that don't quietly break at 2am. I'd rather build a working GDPR audit simulator than write a slide about why GDPR matters — so that's what I did. Eight projects, all real, all on GitHub, all things I'd happily walk you through line by line.

Outside of data: I'm in Berlin, learning German at a pace best described as "determined," and always up for a conversation about AI, governance, or why my model only hit 91.2% and not higher.
